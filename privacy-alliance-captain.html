Alliance Captain Privacy Policy

  1. Information we collect and how we use it

    1. Personally Identifiable Information We do not collect any identifying information from you.
    2. Voice recording When you invoke Alliance Captain through a voice assistant such as a Google Home, a text-based interpretation of your speech is sent to our servers. This information is never stored.
    3. Logs and analytics We do collect anonymous usage data. This information is stored for debugging purposes and for analyzing which features are being used to help inform future development effort.

  2. Data sharing

    We do not share any information we collect with any third parties.
